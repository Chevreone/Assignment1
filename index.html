Tutorial about soccer



First:

Find a team. It could be with friends or a soccer league in your community

Second:

Acquire the items for soccer. You will need soccer cleats, soccer ball, short, jersey or any (sport clothing) and goals

Third:

Find the best position for you. Usually, it is better for the try all the position on field. But main abilities you need are: Goalkeeping: If you are attentive and have lot of flexibility with have a good reaction. Defense: Strong and can stop with shoulder strengh. Midfielder: Overall, good with the ball, has better control and make decision at a good speed. Striker: It is better to have a good view of open space. Also, they need strongest kicker of the ball.



Four:

Bring the soccer balll and play soccer.
